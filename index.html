About Me

I graduated from Tarlac State University with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology in 2017. Following my graduation, I embarked on my professional journey, beginning as a Customer Service Representative at Sutherland Global Services for a period of 3 months. Subsequently, I transitioned to a role as an IT Support Specialist at Zantua's General Merchandising, where I dedicated nearly 5 years of my career. During my tenure, I acquired extensive knowledge in computer hardware and software, as well as foundational skills in networking. Driven by my aspiration to become a proficient programmer, I enrolled in KodeGo Bootcamp, where I completed an intensive 3-month program. Throughout this bootcamp, I delved into various programming languages and technologies including JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL, Bootstrap, as well as gained exposure to basic concepts in React and Laravel. Upon completion of the bootcamp, I remained committed to expanding my skill set and pursued independent learning initiatives to further refine my expertise. My ultimate goal is to establish myself as a competent web developer, leveraging my accumulated knowledge and experiences to contribute effectively to the field.
